About Jurisvista LLP

Jurisvista LLP, Advocates & Solicitors, is a full-service, multi-jurisdictional Indian law firm founded in 2025. Our roots trace back to the established legacy of G.S. Gangwar & Associates (Founded in 2002). This transition combines decades of established expertise with a renewed vision for innovative, client-centric solutions driven by integrity, professionalism, and strategic thinking.

About Us

Jurisvista LLP, Advocates & Solicitors
