JURISVISTA LLP

Advocates & Solicitors

Jurisvista LLP, Advocates & Solicitors, is a full-service, multi-jurisdictional Indian law firm founded in 2025. Our roots trace back to the established legacy of G.S. Gangwar & Associates (Founded in 2002). This transition combines decades of established expertise with a renewed vision for innovative, client-centric solutions driven by integrity, professionalism, and strategic thinking.

Our offices: Strategically located in Delhi NCR and Mumbai, and an extensive nationwide network, our team offers seamless legal services and a thorough understanding of India's complex legal and business environment.

Multi-jurisdictional law firm: We serve a wide spectrum of clients from individuals and start-ups to large corporations and multinational enterprises providing tailored, practical, and sustainable legal solutions across diverse sectors.
