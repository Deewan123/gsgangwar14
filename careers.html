Internships

At Jurisvista LLP, Advocates & Solicitors, our internship programme runs throughout the year and is designed to provide aspiring legal professionals with practical exposure, mentorship, and a strong foundation for their future careers.

We collaborate with placement committees of leading law schools across the country, as well as directly process individual internship applications. Our interns get the opportunity to work alongside our lawyers, gaining firsthand insight into diverse areas of practice and the workings of a modern law firm.

If you are a student from a law school where we already have a coordinated internship programme, please contact your Campus Internship or Placement Committee for further details.
