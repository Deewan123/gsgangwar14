Services

At Jurisvista, we offer a comprehensive range of legal services across diverse sectors, blending in-depth expertise with practical, business-oriented solutions. Our team of lawyers brings specialized knowledge and experience in their respective fields, ensuring that every matter is handled with precision, strategy, and dedication.

Our Practice Areas

We are committed to delivering the highest standards of legal excellence, providing clients with tailored advice, effective representation, and results-driven solutions. At Jurisvista, we believe in going beyond conventional advocacy, combining innovation, foresight, and professionalism to serve our clients best interests.

Arbitration & Dispute Resolution

At JurisVista LLP, we handle both domestic and international arbitration, providing end-to-end support from drafting arbitration agreements to representing clients in proceedings. Our expertise includes commercial and contractual disputes, enforcement of domestic and foreign arbitral awards, and alternative dispute resolution methods such as conciliation and mediation.

Corporate & Commercial Laws

Our team advises clients on all aspects of corporate governance, general corporate matters, and commercial compliance, including company formation, regulatory compliance, mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures, corporate restructuring, due diligence, and shareholder disputes. We also handle corporate litigation, ensuring clients receive practical, business-focused legal solutions.

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)

Our IPR practice helps clients protect and enforce their intellectual property, including patents, trademarks, copyrights, and designs. We assist with licensing agreements, IP commercialization, litigation, dispute resolution, and enforcement against infringement.

Insolvency & Bankruptcy

We focus on corporate restructuring and debt recovery under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). Our services include advising creditors and debtors on resolution plans, representing clients in NCLT and other forums, and handling corporate rescue and liquidation processes.

Consumer Law

We protect consumer rights and ensure fair market practices. Our team handles disputes involving defective goods, deficient services, unfair trade practices, false advertising, and e-commerce issues. We appear before consumer commissions at all levels, including the National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (NCDRC), delivering effective strategies to secure compensation and enforce accountability.

Real Estate & Property Law

We offer comprehensive support in real estate and property matters, covering property acquisition, leasing agreements, RERA compliance, land acquisition, zoning, infrastructure projects, and real estate litigation and recovery.

Employment & Labour Laws

We advise and represent clients on employment and industrial relations matters, including employment contracts, HR compliance, industrial disputes, service matters, workplace investigations, compliance audits, and representation before labour tribunals.

Constitutional & Administrative Law

Our public law practice covers writ petitions, public interest litigation, regulatory compliance, and government contracts. We represent clients before courts and tribunals, ensuring protection of their rights and adherence to legal frameworks.

Banking, Finance & Insurance

We provide legal support across banking, financial services, and insurance law, covering loan agreements, recovery, securitization, regulatory compliance, dispute resolution, insurance claims and litigation, structured finance, and project financing.

Securities Market & SEBI Law

Our team provides specialized advisory and litigation services in capital markets and securities law. We handle SEBI compliance audits, public offerings, IPOs, insider trading and market manipulation investigations, and representation before SEBI, stock exchanges, and appellate authorities. We also advise on corporate governance and disclosure obligations for listed companies.

White-Collar Crimes & Investigations

We handle corporate investigations and white-collar crime matters, including fraud, mismanagement, corporate governance violations, cybercrime, and digital investigations. We represent clients before investigative agencies and courts, providing strategic and effective solutions.

Matrimonial & Family Law

We provide sensitive and expert guidance in family law matters, including divorce, maintenance, alimony, child custody, adoption, property disputes, and settlements. Our approach balances compassion with strategic advocacy to achieve favorable outcomes.

Mines, Minerals & Infrastructure

We provide legal support in natural resources and infrastructure projects, including mining leases, project approvals, infrastructure contracts, dispute resolution, and environmental compliance and litigation.

Debt Recovery & Securitization

We assist banks, financial institutions, and corporates in debt recovery and securitization matters, including recovery proceedings under DRT and SARFAESI, structuring recovery and securitization arrangements, and advising on negotiation and litigation strategies.

Contracts & Deeds

Our team ensures robust contractual frameworks for clients, covering drafting and vetting agreements for commercial transactions, conveyances, deeds, property documentation, and negotiation and risk mitigation strategies.

Startup Support

We offer specialized Startup Support, assisting emerging businesses with entity formation, compliance, funding documentation, ESOP structuring, IP protection, and commercial agreements. Our goal is to provide startups with the right legal foundation to innovate, grow, and scale confidently.

Maritime & Aviation Law

We advise clients on maritime and aviation matters, including shipping contracts, charter agreements, aviation leasing, regulatory compliance, liability claims, and dispute resolution in domestic and international forums. Our team provides strategic solutions tailored to the complex regulatory frameworks of these specialized sectors.

Jurisvista LLP is a premier full-service Indian law firm specializing in corporate law, litigation, arbitration, and regulatory compliance. With offices in Delhi NCR and Mumbai, we provide innovative legal solutions backed by deep expertise and unwavering commitment to client success.
